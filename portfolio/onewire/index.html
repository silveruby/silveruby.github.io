About the Company

OneWire is a website that focuses on matching people with new financial career opportunities. I was hired as their 2nd User Experience Designer with the goal to increase user conversions (getting more sign-ups from both job-seekers and employers). My main responsibilities were designing and prototyping new features for the site.

Both the development team and product team worked together using the scrum process.

Work Examples

My biggest project at the company was redesigning the public site. I worked with stakeholders to identify content assets and created overall content strategy.

At the prototyping phase, I took the role as front-end developer and implemented the HTML, CSS and Javascript for prototypes and the actual production release.
