About the Project

This was a fun 10 hour project that my group and I did for Amped Hack Day. The challenge was to use some sort of Open Web technology to promote Open Web. Our group consisted of a graphic designer, two programmers and interaction designer (me). We decided to learn SVG to implement the idea of merging the old classical art with new technology. As a group, we decided to use classical arts from three great Renaissance artists: Raphael, Michelangelo and Leonardo.

I was the one that came up with the idea and did the SVG image manipulation.
