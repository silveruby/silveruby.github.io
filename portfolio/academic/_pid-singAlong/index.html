Welcome to SingAlong!

The goal of this site is to allow anyone to be able to sync any lyrics or text for existing YouTube video. No additional software is required. Just search for a video, copy 'n paste in lyrics and sync them!

You do not need to play around with lyrics offset time to sync the lyrics. Simply sync the lyrics by scrolling line by line in the bar bellow as the video plays. For example, when the line "You are my sunshine" is played in the video, click 'Next' to scroll to the next line.
